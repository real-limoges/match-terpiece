match-terpiece


Match-terpiece is a simple app that solves a simple problem. Many people love abstract art, but can't quite put their finger on what it is they love about a piece.


Match-terpiece aims to solve this. It's a simple as uploading a photo of a piece you like, and you'll get a few recommendations of pieces you might also be interested in.

Not convinced? Try uploading a photo of one of your favorite abstract art pieces - it'll only take a couple seconds to come up with a few recommendations for you!
