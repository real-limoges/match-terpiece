match-terpiece

Match-terpiece is a simple app that solves a simple problem.

Have you ever seen a piece of art you like, but can't put your finger on what you like about it?


Match-terpiece aims to solve this. It's a simple as uploading a photo of a piece you like.

Upload a photo of a piece you like, and Match-terpiece will show you 10 similar style pieces in less than a second.
